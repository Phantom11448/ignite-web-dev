3FS — GAME RULES

Objective

Be the first player to get rid of all your cards — hand, chosen deck, and blind cards.

Card Order (Low → High)

456 789 JQK A3 ★

Special Cards

  • 2 — Reset. You play again. Opponent only needs to beat a 2.
  • 10 — Auto-blow. Play pile goes to blown deck. You go again.
  • 3 — Highest card. Unbeatable. Nothing beats a 3.
  • 4 of a kind — Any 4 cards of the same number in the pile triggers a blow.

How to Play

  • Each player starts with 10 cards. Pick your 3 best for your chosen deck. Play with the remaining 7.
  • Play one card (or multiple of the same number) per turn.
  • You must match or beat the last card played.
  • If you can't beat it, tap the pile to pick it up.
  • Draw from the pot when your hand drops below 3.

End Game

  • When pot is empty, tap your chosen deck to pick it up.
  • When chosen is empty, tap blind deck — draws one card at a time.
  • First player with no cards wins!
